Features

Dashboard

With an excellent welcome panel with the user’s name you will find many interesting features in the dashboard. All these features are good enough to have a basic idea of your shop status. At the start of the page you will find category, medicine items, executive no and invoice amount. You will find 4 statistics chart which are day-to-day sale statistics, day-to-day sold product list, also sale statistics for 10 and 30 days pattern.

Manage Medicine

Couple of functionality falls into this category such as medicine list, shortage of medicines, list of medicine near to expire date and expired list. All these lists can be edited, deleted and viewed just by clicking a button. This section performs the function of your inventory.

Medicine Category, Executive and supplier

The main job of these three section is to add medicine category for the software to use. Add, delete or save executive & supplier information for future references.

Executive Panel

Executive panel is restricted to many features like they can’t add or delete medicine, medicine category, and executive or supplier list. They can sell, create invoice and view medicine lists and date wise report.

Point of sale

In this section you can create invoice for selling. You have to search for a specific product either from a list of category or directly by product name. You can add tax in the invoice. There will also be expiry date and available quantity of the product.

Search Medicine

Managing a pharmacy is a tough job. The most difficult task is to find the correct medicine in a short time. FEITS introduces the best way to search for medicine using form Generic name or Medicine class name.

Purchase Medicine

It is similar to the point of sale section. Instead of selling, this segment keeps track of all the purchases incurred for your pharmacy.

Manage Expense

This particular section lets you manage your expenses and keep track of your hard earned money. It will also be easy to determine where to spend your money for better shop management.

Report

This option allows you to generate daily sales reports or from certain date to another. Also you can keep track of your executives. You can find out which executive sells what and when.

Facilities
