About Us

Message of the Managing Director

Far-East IT Solutions Limited was built at a time when there was already a huge competition that existed within the software industry. So many software businesses were formed and the market was saturated. We started our journey considering the situation but with a clear vision in mind. We wanted to stand out from the crowd. We could only achieve that by achieving client satisfaction and turn the clients into a loyal client base. Now after a year, I believe we have achieved that by a fair extent. We have served many national and international clients and they are happy with the products and services that we have provided. Our employees are satisfied with our company’s offerings and we work as a team to achieve new milestones. Their dream is our dream. And together we strive to achieve greater success. We are here to stay and not just to fade away with time. For that, we have to prove our worth time and time again. That’s what our challenge is and we love to take new challenges as individuals and as an organization.

Our Mission

Far-East IT solutions limited was founded with a rebellious spirit and a sky-high objective: to offer the most user-friendly software services and web solutions at a revolutionary price, while leading the way for socially conscious business.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the change that the IT industry of Bangladesh is in need of. We want to develop software that will make the life easier. By fully utilizing the possibilities that we have as an emerging nation, we want to be the leader of the IT industry by changing the trends and bringing the products and services that would create an impact in the life of the people both nationally and internationally.
